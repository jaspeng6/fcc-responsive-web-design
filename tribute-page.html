David Goggins

The toughest man on earth

David Goggins is the toughest man on Earth, earning acolades from many differnet hundred mile triathlons and being the only man to participate 3 iron mans in 3 weeks, withstanding hell.

David Goggins: A Journey of Unbreakable Resilience

  • 1975 - Born on February 17th
  • 1983 - Faces a challenging childhood marked by poverty, racism, and health issues
  • 1994 - Enlists in the U.S. Air Force
  • 2001 - Joins the Navy SEALs
  • 2005 - Completes the rigorous SEAL training three times
  • 2006 - Sets the Guinness World Record for the most pull-ups in 24 hours
  • 2010 - Completes the Badwater 135 ultramarathon, the toughest foot race in the world
  • 2012 - Releases his memoir, "Can't Hurt Me," sharing his inspirational life story
  • 2018 - Begins the "4x4x48 Challenge," running 4 miles every 4 hours for 48 hours
  • 2020 - Continues to inspire millions through his motivational speaking and relentless pursuit of greatness
  • Present - Serves as a living testament to the power of resilience, determination, and an unbreakable mindset
